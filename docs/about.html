About Praevius: AI Cost Control for Construction Subcontractors

20 years of construction cost management expertise, now powered by AI.

The Story

Praevius (from Latin “praevius” meaning “going before”) was born from a simple observation: construction subcontractors are drowning in spreadsheets while enterprise platforms price them out of modern cost control.

After two decades working as a quantity surveyor across the UK, Australia, and Poland—handling everything from 500K fitouts to 50M commercial developments—the pattern became undeniable. Specialty trade contractors, the backbone of every construction project, were consistently:

  • Losing 3-5% margins to poor variation tracking
  • Wasting 10+ hours monthly on manual billing cycles
  • Discovering cost overruns weeks after they could have acted
  • Being priced out of tools designed for general contractors

Praevius is the solution: professional-grade cost control without enterprise complexity. Simple enough to learn in hours, powerful enough to replace spreadsheet chaos.

Why Quantity Surveying Software Needed to Change

Traditional quantity surveying software was designed for enterprise clients with dedicated IT teams and training budgets. That left mid-market contractors with two options: expensive platforms that required weeks of training, or spreadsheets that created more problems than they solved.

The construction industry loses billions annually to:

  • Spreadsheet errors - 88% of spreadsheets contain errors (KPMG study)
  • Poor cash flow visibility - Only 12% of contractors get paid on time
  • Missed variations - Change orders represent 8-14% of project value
  • Reactive cost control - Problems discovered weeks after they occurred

Praevius bridges this gap: professional-grade construction cost control software with mid-market pricing and learning curves measured in hours, not weeks.
