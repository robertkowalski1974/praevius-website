Core Capabilities

Real-Time Budget Tracking

See your budget vs. actual costs the moment data changes. No more waiting for month-end reports to discover problems. Dashboard views show project health at a glance with traffic-light status indicators.

  • Live cost performance index (CPI)
  • Budget consumption tracking
  • Variance alerts and thresholds
  • Multi-project portfolio view

AI-Powered Reporting

Generate professional variance reports in minutes, not hours. Our AI analyzes patterns, identifies anomalies, and explains the “why” behind your numbers in plain English.

  • Automated variance explanations
  • Trend analysis and forecasting
  • Executive summary generation
  • Export to PDF, Word, or share live

S-Curve Forecasting

Earned Value Management made practical. See where your project is headed, not just where it’s been. Plan interventions before problems escalate.

  • Planned vs. actual vs. forecast curves
  • Schedule Performance Index (SPI)
  • Estimate at Completion (EAC) projections
  • What-if scenario modeling

Change Order Management

Track every variation from inception to approval to payment. Never leave money on the table again. Change orders account for 8-14% of project costs—capture them all.

  • Variation register with status tracking
  • Automated entitlement flagging
  • Impact assessment tools
  • Approval workflow automation

Progress Claim Automation

Generate accurate progress claims in minutes. Centralized dashboard eliminates hunting through spreadsheets and rechecking every line item manually.

  • Schedule of values tracking
  • Percentage complete calculations
  • Retainage management
  • Claim history and audit trail

Seamless Integrations

Connect to the tools you already use. Praevius syncs with accounting, storage, and project management platforms for a single source of truth.

  • Xero, QuickBooks, Sage
  • Google Drive, SharePoint, OneDrive
  • Procore (for GC collaboration)
  • Open API for custom connections

Industry-Standard Variance Indicators

Instant project health visibility using the traffic light system every construction professional understands.

On Budget

Variance within ±5%. Project performing as planned. Continue current trajectory.

Watch

Variance 5-10%. Early warning. Investigate and plan corrective action.

Over Budget

Variance exceeds 10%. Immediate attention required. Intervention needed.
