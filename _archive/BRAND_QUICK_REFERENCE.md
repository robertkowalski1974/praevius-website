# Praevius Brand Guidelines - Quick Reference

## Brand Essence
**Praevius** (Latin: "going before") - Proactive cost control intelligence.

**Tagline:** "See costs before they spiral"

## Primary Colors
- **Orange:** `#FF9900` (RGB: 255, 153, 0) - CTAs, highlights, brand accent
- **Charcoal:** `#2C2C2C` (RGB: 44, 44, 44) - Text, dark backgrounds
- **White:** `#FFFFFF` - Light backgrounds, reverse text

## Secondary Colors
- **Light Gray:** `#F0F0F0` - Secondary backgrounds
- **Medium Gray:** `#757575` - Secondary text, borders
- **Dark Gray:** `#404040` - Alternative dark

## Typography
- **Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Weights:** 400, 500, 600, 700, 800
- **Headings:** 700-800 weight
- **Body:** 400 weight
- **Technical/Data:** Monaco, monospace
